# Obsidian home

Use this vault as a Git-synced personal wiki.

## What goes where

- `00_Inbox` for quick capture
- `10_Concepts` for durable ideas
- `20_Projects` for active work
- `30_Decisions` for why we chose something
- `40_Logs` for dated progress notes
- `99_Private` for local-only sensitive notes

## Writing habit

- Start messy in `00_Inbox`.
- Promote only the durable part into the right folder.
- Keep each note small enough to find again later.
